- **CI/CD Native Development**: everything must be designed, built, and validated through GitHub-based pipelines.
- **Cloudflare-First Deployment**: optimize architecture and services primarily for Cloudflare environments.
- **Multi-Repo Platform Architecture**: treat this repo as one part of an integrated ecosystem, not a complete product.

- **Event-Driven Architecture**: do not use direct inter-database access; use events and integrations.
- **Respect prior completion**: completed Civic modules should be extended, not reinvented.
